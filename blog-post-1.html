SUSTAINABILITY

Crafted by Hand, Designed for the Planet

December 5, 2025

At Eyelix, we believe that luxury should not come at the expense of the planet's future. Every pair of glasses we create is a harmonious blend of traditional craftsmanship and modern ecological responsibility.

Our "Carbon Negative" Philosophy

We are committed to being more than just "carbon neutral"; we aim for "carbon negative." This means we strive to remove more carbon from the atmosphere than we emit throughout our entire supply chain, from raw materials to delivery. It is a challenging journey, but one we are determined to pursue.

Recycled Materials: The Heart of Our Product

Material choice is central to Eyelix's sustainability philosophy. We prioritize sourcing materials that have the lowest possible environmental impact.

  • Recycled Metal: Significantly reduces the need for new resource extraction.
  • Bio-Acetate: A polymer of biological origin that is capable of decomposition.
  • Plastic-Free Packaging: All packaging is made from recycled materials, continuing our eco-friendly promise.
"Craftsmanship is not just about making a beautiful product; it's about investing time and meticulous care into a sustainable future."

Every Eyelix product is a testament that responsible, sustainable fashion can go hand-in-hand. We invite you to join us on this journey of change.
